Department of Information Technology (NIET)

About Department

The Department of Technology and Innovation is dedicated to pushing the boundaries of technology and fostering innovation in various domains. Our mission is to drive progress, create solutions, and empower individuals and businesses through cutting-edge technology and creative thinking.

Our Goals

  • Advance research in emerging technologies.
  • Promote innovation through collaboration and partnerships.
  • Provide education and training opportunities in tech-related fields.
  • Support startups and entrepreneurs in bringing their ideas to life.
  • Contribute to the growth and competitiveness of our region's tech industry.

Meet Our Team

  • Researchers
  • Software Developers
  • Innovators
  • Educators
  • Entrepreneurs in Residence
